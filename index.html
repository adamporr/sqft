Residential Floor Area Statistics Calculator

This tool computes summary statistics for the residential floor area of parcels within a radius of a center location. Coverage is limited to Franklin County, Ohio. Enter the radius below, then double-click on the map or enter an address to define the center. A blue circle indicating the area of interest will appear on the map. After a few moments, the residential parcels will appear on the map and the statistics will appear below.
